The Alchemist is one of my favorite books. I discovered it in 2015 while searching for a present for my dear friend Draga. I was first impressed by the bright colored cover that stood out among the other books and then by the intriguing plot. the main lessons upon this book are: Even if we feel lost during our journey we must always fight for our dreams and don't forget that we are not alone. The Universe is there, ready to cooperate with us in order to make our wish comes true. This is a lesson that I will never forget and that I try to implement in my every day life.
